--- layout: null section-type: faq title: FAQ --- ## FAQ Frequently Asked Questions QUESTION: How can AI drive efficiency for the common good? ANSWER: Recently Open AI and Microsoft have provided secure LLM for both knowledge retrieval and secure coding. We take an egalitarian approach to assistants and workflows. Chat is table stakes and the population expects it. QUESTION: How can I enhance my government Sharepoint site with a chatbot? ANSWER: With Azure, Sharepoint files can be migrated to to blob storage through REST API. There the files can be used by the RAG algorithm to search for answers. QUESTION: Do I need to be a programmer to benefit from AI workflows? ANSWER: No, the content that is managed through an AI powered workflow does not only benefit programmers, but any analyst who wants insights in data ahead of reviewing. AI empowered workflows can trigger monitoring. Or simply make data more accessible to the public by posting updated analytics. QUESTION: What type of tasks can AI accomplish? ANSWER: AI can perform internet research on topics. It can access XSS or API and prepare briefings that assists people who fact check, edit, and publish.
